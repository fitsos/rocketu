People want to make charitable contributions they just don't know where to start, or they start and loose steam, ending up with a series of disconnected charitable contributions with no connected theme, short-stopping its fullest potential. Altruist ensures that this is a thing of the past. You select an annual donation target and we bring fun, diverse events to your attention, ensuring that you are affecting what's nearest to your heart as often as possible. And once you are finished, we will maintain your profile's timeline so that you can reminisce.
